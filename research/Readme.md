# Foundational Research

This directory contains all the foundational academic research, exploratory notes, proofs-of-concept, and paper drafts that underpin the concepts and polished documentation found in the `/docs` directory.

## Directory Contents

- **/AirAccount**: Core research on the AirAccount system, including paper drafts, design notes, and evaluations of other AA solutions.
- **/CNXConnect**: Research related to the CNX-Connect concept.
- **/CryptoMart**: Notes on the CryptoMart idea.
- **/HyperCapital**: In-depth research on the HyperCapital social capital framework.
- **/Knowledge**: A repository for foundational knowledge, including candidate journals, paper templates, and publication plans.
- **/Mycelium**: Documents related to the broader Mycelium Protocol and MushroomDAO ecosystem.
- **/OnionModel**: Research into the Onion security model for layered, context-aware security.
- **/OpenPNTs**: Exploratory work on a decentralized loyalty points system.
- **/Society&Complexity**: Notes on societal structures and complexity theory.
- **/SuperPaymaster**: Core research and paper drafts for the SuperPaymaster gas abstraction service.
