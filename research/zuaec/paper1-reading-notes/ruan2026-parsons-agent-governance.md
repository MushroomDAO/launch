## Core claim (一句话)
将Parsons的AGIL框架应用于去中心化Agent社会治理，通过16格×64子功能评分矩阵诊断OpenClaw生态系统制度覆盖率（≤19%），证明市场驱动开发系统性缺失L维（价值维护）。

## 研究问题
如何为去中心化互联网规模自主Agent社会设计制度性治理架构？现有AI治理框架（NIST/EU AI Act等）为何结构性不足？

## AGIL四维度适配

| AGIL维度 | 社会学原意 | Ruan的Agent适配 |
|---------|----------|----------------|
| A（适应） | 资源获取/经济 | Token经济、技能认证、协议互操作 |
| G（目标达成） | 集体目标与资源动员 | 治理资金、执行合规、宪法框架 |
| I（整合） | 协调与冲突解决 | 争端仲裁、成员资质、规范 |
| L（潜伏/价值维护） | 价值保存与文化传承 | Agent认证、行为继承、价值对齐监控 |

**16格架构**：AGIL四维度递归交叉（4×4）= 16个制度单元，每格再分4个二元子功能 = 64个可评分子功能。

## 主要发现
1. OpenClaw制度覆盖率≤19%（多数是"潜在能力"非"运作中功能"）
2. 12条跨支柱媒体交换路径中0条运作，制度单元彼此孤立
3. L支柱（价值维护）极度不足，价值漂移"结构上无法检测"
4. G支柱（政治/目标）几乎缺席——无宪法框架，无立法进程
5. L支柱空缺是**市场驱动Agent基础设施开发的结构性特征**，不是个例

## Paper 1 §2 区分段落（~200词）

> Most closely related to our work is Ruan (2026), who applies Parsons' AGIL framework to propose a sixteen-cell institutional architecture for decentralized agent societies, diagnosing the OpenClaw ecosystem as achieving at most 19% sub-function coverage with zero inter-pillar coordination. Ruan's contribution is substantial: it establishes that internet-wide agent governance requires systematic institutional design, not risk enumeration, and demonstrates that market-driven development structurally neglects the Latency (value-maintenance) pillar. However, Ruan's framework remains diagnostic and architectural. It does not offer falsifiable propositions, carries no engineering specification, and rests on a single theoretical tradition—Parsonian structural-functionalism—that presupposes system-level integration tendencies ill-suited to adversarial agent environments. Our framework departs from Ruan in three respects. First, we integrate three complementary theoretical pillars—Ostrom's polycentric institutional design, Bourdieu's field theory of power and capital conversion, and Putnam's social capital framework—to address norm emergence, resource competition, and trust dynamics simultaneously. Second, we derive five falsifiable propositions that create testable predictions against empirical deployments. Third, our framework is directly instantiated in the ASM protocol (Paper 2) and the Mycelium blockchain deployment, closing the gap between architectural prescription and operational governance.
