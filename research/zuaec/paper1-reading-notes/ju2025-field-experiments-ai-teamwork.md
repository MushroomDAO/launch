# [Ju & Aral 2025] Collaborating with AI Agents: Field Experiments on Teamwork, Productivity, and Performance

**Citation**: Ju, H., & Aral, S. (2025). Collaborating with AI Agents: Field Experiments on Teamwork, Productivity, and Performance. *arXiv:2503.18238*
**arXiv**: https://arxiv.org/abs/2503.18238
**Cluster**: Human-AI Collaboration — Empirical Evidence
**Status**: 摘要已读，全文待阅

## Core claim (一句话)
田野实验（n=2234）发现人机团队提升50%产出，但同时导致沟通任务化（+25%任务型消息，-18%人际型）和输出同质化。

## Key findings (摘要层级)
1. **+50% 产出提升**（具体任务类型待全文确认）
2. **通信模式改变**：+25%任务导向，-18%人际型沟通
3. **同质化风险**：AI辅助导致团队输出趋同
4. **机构**：Sinan Aral是MIT管理学院社会科学顶级学者，2,234人样本是大规模实证

## Relation to our framework
