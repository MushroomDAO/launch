# [Xi et al. 2024] The Rise and Potential of Large Language Model Based Agents: A Survey

**Citation**: Xi, Z., Chen, W., Guo, X., He, W., Ding, Y., Hong, B., ... & Gui, T. (2024). The Rise and Potential of Large Language Model Based Agents: A Survey. *arXiv:2309.07864*
**arXiv**: https://arxiv.org/abs/2309.07864
**Cluster**: Foundational Survey — covers all clusters
**Status**: Skim only (待全文阅读)

## Core claim (一句话)
LLM-based agents have three core components (brain/perception/action), can form "Agent Society" with emergent social behaviors including cooperation, competition, and social norm formation.

## Key findings (摘要层级)
1. 三层架构：Brain（LLM核心）+ Perception（多模态感知）+ Action（工具调用/具身行动）
2. Agent Society专章：多个LLM Agent可涌现合作/竞争/社会规范
3. 覆盖2022-2024年全部主要框架（AutoGPT/AutoGen/MetaGPT/CAMEL等）
4. 对比评估：多种benchmark测试不同Agent能力维度

## Relation to our framework
- **L3诊断**：该综述把Agent定义为"能力容器"（Brain+Perception+Action架构）→ 恰好是我们范式转换的起点
- **Paper 1 §2**：作为"现有综述的代表性工作"引用，说明主流定义缺失社会角色维度
- **关键缺口**：该综述的"Agent Society"章节描述的是行为涌现，而非制度性嵌入 → 这正是我们的差异化点
