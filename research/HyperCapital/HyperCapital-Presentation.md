## Slide 2: The Core Problem: The Twilight of Platform Capitalism

*   **The Current Paradigm:** Platforms like Google & Meta create immense value by providing services.
*   **The Hidden Cost:** This value is fueled by the systematic extraction of user **Social Capital** (data, attention, influence).
*   **The Structural Flaw:** A profound **value distribution inequality**. Users create the value, but platforms capture the profit.
    *   *Alphabet's 2023 Ad Revenue: $237.8 Billion (77.8% of total)*
*   **The Consequence:** Innovation is stifled, privacy is eroded, and users are alienated from their own digital creations.

## Slide 3: The Research Question & Contribution

*   **Our Core Question:**
    > How can we design a new digital economic paradigm to replace the current extractive model, and fairly value and distribute the social capital we all create?

*   **Our Contribution: HyperCapital**
    1.  **A New Theory:** Defining social capital as a programmable, tradable economic asset.
    2.  **A New Model:** Proposing a decentralized, collaborative economy focused on **well-being**, not just profit.
    3.  **A New Path:** Validated through a rigorous **Design Science Research** approach.

---

## Slide 4: Theoretical Foundation: From Social Capital to HyperCapital

*   **Classical Social Capital (Bourdieu, Coleman):**
    *   Valuable but... **intangible**, **context-dependent**, and **unquantifiable**.
    *   Easily extracted by platforms.

*   **HyperCapital (Our Theory):**
    *   A digital, **verifiable**, and **quantifiable** representation of social capital.
    *   **Core Idea:** Transforming social contributions from a liability (to be extracted) into an **asset** (to be owned).

| Feature | Social Capital | HyperCapital |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Ownership** | Ambiguous | Clear (Cryptographic) |
| **Quantification** | Difficult | Protocol-based |
| **Portability** | Low | High (Tradable) |
| **Goal** | Social Cohesion | **Economic Well-being** |

## Slide 7: The Value Proposition: A Win-Win-Win

*   **For Users/Customers:**
    *   Their digital labor is **rewarded**.
    *   They gain access to **authentic information**.
    *   They become **owners** in the economies they help create.

## Slide 9: Discussion: Challenges & The Path Forward

*   **Key Challenges We Acknowledge:**
    1.  **Scalability:** How to grow from a local community to a global network?
    2.  **Value Anchoring:** How to peg HyperCapital to real-world value?
    3.  **Governance Risks:** The classic problems of DAOs (e.g., voter apathy, plutocracy).
    4.  **Ethical Boundaries:** The risk of "over-quantifying" social interactions.

*   **Our Response:**
    *   We propose **mitigation strategies** at the protocol level, such as non-tradable reputation badges to reward pure altruism.
