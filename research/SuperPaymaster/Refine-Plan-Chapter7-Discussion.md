## 精炼方案: 第七章 讨论 (Discussion)

### 当前状态评估

第七章内容详尽，但可以大幅精简，使其论点更锐利，更符合期刊论文的篇幅要求。

### 修改核心目标

将本章从一个宽泛的讨论，**压缩并重构为一个高度凝练的总结与展望**。严格遵循您提出的“缩减为1/3”的要求，只保留最核心的论点。

### 具体修改建议

1.  **重写7.1节，聚焦RQ回应：** 将 `7.1 Research Questions Validation Summary` 彻底重写为一个**单独、精悍的段落**。直接、清晰地声明每个RQ是如何被评估结果所回答的。
    *   **示例：** “*Our evaluation provides strong, convergent evidence answering our three research questions. For RQ1 (cost/complexity), our quantitative benchmarking demonstrated a statistically significant reduction in steps (70.1%) and cost (30.0%). For RQ2 (cognitive load), HCI-informed expert assessment validated our 'Gas Card' metaphor as an effective abstraction. Finally, for RQ3 (technical architecture), our successful prototype implementation and positive expert feedback confirmed the architecture's feasibility and robustness.*”

2.  **合并并精简7.2节（Implications）：** 将理论和实践意义合并，用要点（bullet points）的形式呈现，只保留最高层次的贡献。
    *   **Implications for Research:**
        *   Provides a DSR case study applying HCI theory to solve a core blockchain usability problem.
        *   Offers a validated design pattern (“Gas Card” metaphor) for abstracting complex technical concepts.
    *   **Implications for Practice:**
        *   Delivers an open-source framework for building user-friendly dApps.
        *   Presents a competitive market model that can lower transaction costs across the ecosystem.

3.  **合并并精简7.3节（Limitations & Future Work）：** 同样，合并局限性和未来工作，直接陈述，避免过多解释。
    *   **Limitations:** Acknowledge the primary limitations: the evaluation was a controlled experiment, not a large-scale longitudinal study, and the expert panel was limited in size.
    *   **Future Work:** Directly state the logical next steps based on the limitations: (1) Conduct a longitudinal study in a production environment; (2) Perform large-scale usability tests with non-expert users; (3) Develop formal models to test economic security against sophisticated attacks.

### 预期效果

修改后的第七章将变得非常紧凑和强大。它会快速地向读者总结研究的核心贡献、理论与实践意义，并诚实地指出局限与未来方向。整个章节的篇幅将被有效控制在原来的三分之一左右，完全符合顶级期刊对讨论部分“精炼、深刻”的要求。
