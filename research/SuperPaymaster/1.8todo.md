### 评估 (Evaluation) 设计

#### System Usability Scale (SUS) Questionaries
Brooke, J. (1986). System Usability Scale: A quick and dirty usability scale. In Usability evaluation in industry [48].
Define Contens:10 items, where users rate their experience on a scale from 1 (strongly disagree) to 5 (strongly agree).
Example Items:
I think this system is very complex.
I think this system is very easy to use.
Select Users: Choose target users for testing.
Use the System: Allow users to operate the system in a real or simulated environment.
Fill Out the Questionnaire: After using the system, users complete the SUS questionnaire.
Calculate the Score: Compute the SUS score based on user ratings, which ranges from 0 to 100.
Calculation Method: From the odd-numbered items, subtract 1 from the score. From the even-numbered items, subtract 5. Sum all the scores and multiply by 2.5.
Result Interpretation: Score Range: typically ranges from 0 to 100, with higher scores indicating better usability.
Benchmark Comparison: scores can be compared with other systems' scores or with industry standards to evaluate the relative usability of a system.

#### Mathematical method（导师建议）
- Record and calculate the mean, distribution and comparing
- T-test for significance difference
- ANOVA for different group effects
