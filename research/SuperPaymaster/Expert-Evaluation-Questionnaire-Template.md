### **1. Introduction & Research Summary**

**Dear Expert,**

Thank you for your invaluable contribution to our research. We are developing **SuperPaymaster**, a novel system designed to solve the critical problem of high cost and complexity in blockchain transaction fees ("gas"), which is a major barrier to Web3 adoption.

**The Problem:** Gas payments are confusing, expensive, and a primary source of user frustration, rooted in deep Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) challenges.

**Our Solution:** SuperPaymaster is a decentralized gas payment system based on Ethereum's Account Abstraction (ERC-4337). Its core innovation is a **competitive, open market for gas sponsorship** combined with a user-centric design centered on the **"Gas Card" metaphor**, aiming to make gas payments seamless and "invisible" to the end-user.

**Your Role:** We kindly request your expert opinion on the validity and effectiveness of our proposed design, particularly concerning its HCI contributions and technical architecture.
