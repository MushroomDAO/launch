# Spores Plan: Reallocation Revolution
## What are Spores
It is a biology concept about the **seed of Mushroom**, a kind of Mycelium.
A mature spore growth up to a mushroom and release **millions** of Spores in one time.
Every spore will find a place to live and be **intergrowth**.

## What do Spores do?

Building a symbiotic network is a good way, act as a unit of a system, we call this Mycelium Network.
They be Intergrowth with **roots**, **breakdown, collect and transfer nutrition, allocate** to weak and groups, and then **burst** millions of spores what we call a **cycle**.

## What will Spores get?

Act as a** public goods for total ecosystem**.
